6. Weather Outfit Selector

Create a script that suggests what to wear based on the weather. You input a temperature, and the script tells you what to wear.
You should have a form with a text input field and a button. When the button is clicked, the script should suggest what to wear based on the temperature. For example, if it's cold (under 15 degrees), suggest wearing a jacket. If it's warm, but not hot (between 15 and 25 degrees), suggest a jumper. if it's hot (above 25 degrees), suggest shorts.
